ABOUT US


"WE CARE FOR YOU AND YOU WILL EXPERIENCE A HOME AWAY FROM YOUR HOME"

Homeliness is our aim and it's this distinct experience that makes Second Home one of the best hostels in Kochi Located near Ernakulam Marine Drive,right at the centre of Ernakulam's busiest commercial hub , Second Home provedes a Scenic View of the backwaters and the prestine Cochin Harbour. It is surrounded by shopping complexes, boutiques cafes and reastaurants. The Hostel is within 15 minutes drive from Ernakulam Railway station. The Hostel features private and dormitory rooms, a TV Lounge, a Library and a fully equipped Kitchen. Its has 24/7 reception desk and free wifi in spacious lounge area.This is a sustainable property with solar powered hot water, rainwater tanks and a unique design that invites natural light and airflow.
